# Welcome to RubixKube Docs

RubixKube™ is the **Reliability Layer for the AI Era** - an AI-native mesh of agents that prevents downtime, safeguards revenue, and gives peace of mind at scale.

Think of it as your **second brain for infra**:
- Observes and understands your environments like a seasoned SRE
- Diagnoses issues and shows you the blast radius
- Proposes or applies safe fixes before customers notice
- Connects infrastructure failures to **real business impact**

## Beta Software Notice

RubixKube is currently in **Beta**.
That means:
- More stable than Alpha, but still evolving
- Perfect for **testing and evaluation** - do **NOT** use in production yet
- Your feedback shapes the production release

<Warning>
Read our [Beta Disclaimers](/getting-started/disclaimers) before installing RubixKube on any environment.
