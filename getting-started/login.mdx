## Email Login (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Navigate to Login Page

Visit [console.rubixkube.ai](https://console.rubixkube.ai) - you'll be automatically redirected to the login page if you're not already signed in.

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/login-01-welcome-page.png"
    alt="RubixKube login page"
  />
</Frame>

The login page displays:
- **Email address field** - Enter your registered email
- **Continue with Google** - Quick OAuth login with Google
- **Continue with GitHub** - Quick OAuth login with GitHub
- **Sign up link** - For new users who need to create an account

### Step 2: Enter Your Email

Type in the email address you used during signup and click **Continue**.

<Tip>
<strong>Pro Tip:</strong> The login page remembers your last used email address on this device for faster access.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Enter Your Password

After entering your email, you'll be directed to the password entry screen.

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/login-02-enter-password.png"
    alt="Password entry screen"
  />
</Frame>

**On this screen:**
- Your email address is displayed at the top
- Click **"Edit"** if you need to change the email
- Enter your password in the secure field
- Toggle **"Show password"** to verify your entry
- Click **"Forgot password?"** if you need to reset it
- Click **Continue** to log in

<Warning>
<strong>Security Reminder:</strong> Always ensure you're on `console.rubixkube.ai` or `rubixkube.us.auth0.com` before entering your credentials.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

After successful authentication, you'll be redirected to your RubixKube dashboard!

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/login-03-dashboard-onboarding.png"
    alt="RubixKube dashboard after login"
  />
</Frame>

**First-time login?** You'll see the onboarding screen with instructions to:
- Connect your first Kubernetes cluster
- Understand the key features
- Start monitoring your infrastructure

## Social Login (GitHub & Google)

### GitHub Login

1. Click **"Continue with GitHub"** on the login page
2. Authorize RubixKube if this is your first time
3. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

**Benefits:**
- No password to remember
- Automatic SSO if you're already logged into GitHub
- Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication

<Tip>
<strong>For Organizations:</strong> GitHub login respects your organization's SSO policies and 2FA requirements.
</Tip>

### Google Login

1. Click **"Continue with Google"** on the login page
2. Select your Google account from the account picker
3. Grant permissions if this is your first time
4. You'll be automatically logged in

**Benefits:**
- Works seamlessly with Google Workspace accounts
- Single sign-on across all your apps
- Enterprise-grade security

<Tip>
<strong>Google Workspace Users:</strong> Your organization's security policies (2FA, device trust, etc.) automatically apply.
</Tip>

## Password Reset

Forgot your password? No problem!

### Reset Your Password

1. On the password entry screen, click **"Forgot password?"** 2. You'll receive a password reset email at your registered address
3. Click the reset link in the email (valid for 24 hours)
4. Create a new secure password
5. Log in with your new password

### Password Requirements

When creating a new password, ensure it:
- Is at least **8 characters** long
- Contains **lowercase** letters
- Contains **UPPERCASE** letters
- Contains **numbers** - Contains **  special characters** (!@#$%^&*)

## Session Management

### Stay Logged In

RubixKube keeps you logged in for:
- **7 days** on trusted devices (with "Remember me")
- **24 hours** on regular sessions
- **Until you log out** if you manually signed out

### Multiple Sessions

You can be logged in to RubixKube on multiple devices simultaneously:
- Work desktop
- Personal laptop
- Mobile browser

<Note>
For security, any device can log out all other sessions from the <strong>Settings > Security</strong> page.
</Note>

### Automatic Logout

You'll be automatically logged out if:
- You haven't used RubixKube in **7 days** - Your session token expires
- You manually log out
- An admin revokes your access
- You change your password (logs out all other sessions)

## Security Features

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

<Info>
<strong>Coming Soon:</strong> Native 2FA support is being added to RubixKube for enhanced security.
</Info>

Currently, you can enable 2FA through:
- **GitHub** - Enable 2FA on your GitHub account
- **Google** - Enable 2-Step Verification on your Google account

### Device Recognition

RubixKube can recognize trusted devices through:
- Browser fingerprinting
- Biometric authentication (if enabled during signup)
- Device authorization emails

### Suspicious Activity

If RubixKube detects unusual login activity, you'll receive:
- **Email notification** about the login attempt
- **Account lock** if multiple failed attempts occur
- **Verification challenge** from a new device or location

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Password Reset Email Not Received?">
    If you don't receive the password reset email:

    - **Check spam/junk folder** - Reset emails sometimes get filtered
    - **Wait 5 minutes** - Email delivery can occasionally be delayed
    - **Verify email address** - Ensure you entered the correct email
    - **Try again** - Request another reset link
    - **Contact support** - Email connect@rubixkube.ai for assistance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong email or password error">
    **Common causes:**
    - Typo in email or password (check for extra spaces)
    - Caps Lock is enabled
    - Using wrong authentication method (did you sign up with GitHub/Google?)
    - Account hasn't been verified yet

    **Solutions:**
    - Double-check your email address
    - Use "Show password" to verify what you're typing
    - Try the "Forgot password?" flow
    - Check your email for verification link if newly registered
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account locked after multiple attempts">
    After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are temporarily locked for **15 minutes** to prevent brute-force attacks.

    **What to do:**
    - Wait 15 minutes before trying again
    - Use the "Forgot password?" option to reset immediately
    - Contact support if you believe this is an error
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email verified but can't login">
    If you verified your email but still can't log in:
    
    1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
    2. Try an incognito/private browsing window
    3. Ensure you're using the same email you verified
    4. Try a different browser
    5. Contact support at connect@rubixkube.ai
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Social login (GitHub/Google) not working">
    **Common issues:**
    - **Pop-up blocked:** Allow pop-ups for console.rubixkube.ai
    - **Organization restrictions:** Your org may block OAuth apps
    - **Cookies disabled:** Enable third-party cookies temporarily
    - **Account mismatch:** You may have signed up with email but are trying social login

    **Solution:** Use email login as a fallback, then link your social accounts in Settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Infinite redirect loop">
    If you're stuck in a redirect loop:
    
    1. Clear all browser cache and cookies for rubixkube.ai
    2. Close all RubixKube tabs
    3. Open a new incognito/private window
    4. Try logging in again
    5. If persists, try a different browser
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Session expires too quickly">
    If you're being logged out frequently:

    - **Enable "Remember me"** during login
    - **Check browser settings** - Ensure cookies aren't being auto-deleted
    - **Whitelist rubixkube.ai** in your ad blocker
    - **Corporate VPN/Firewall** - May be interfering with session cookies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
