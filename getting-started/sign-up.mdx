## Email Signup (Step-by-Step)

This guide covers the email signup process. For GitHub or Google signup, simply click the respective button and follow the OAuth flow.

### Step 1: Navigate to Sign Up

Visit [console.rubixkube.ai](https://console.rubixkube.ai) and click**"Create an account"**from the login page.

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/login-page.png"
    alt="RubixKube login page with sign-up option"
  />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Enter Your Email

On the**Create Your Account**page, you'll see three options:

1.**Email address**- Enter your work or personal email
2.**Continue with Google**- Instant signup using Google OAuth
3.**Continue with GitHub**- Instant signup using GitHub OAuth

For email signup, enter your email address and click**Continue**.

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/signup-01-create-account.png"
    alt="RubixKube sign-up page showing all authentication options"
  />
</Frame>

<Tip>
**Recommended:**Use your work email address to easily collaborate with your team later.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Create Your Password

Set a strong password for your account. Your password must meet these requirements:

- Minimum** 8 characters**long
- Contains at least** 6 character types**(lowercase, uppercase, numbers, special characters)

The page displays your email address with an option to edit if needed.

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/signup-03-create-password.png"
    alt="Password creation screen with requirements"
  />
</Frame>

<Warning>
**Security Best Practice:**Use a unique password that you don't use elsewhere. Consider using a password manager like 1Password, LastPass, or Bitwarden.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Biometric Authentication (Optional)

After creating your password, RubixKube offers to enable**quick login**using your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition).

You have three options:
- **Continue**- Enable biometric login for this device
- **Remind me later**- Skip for now, ask again next time
- **Not on this device**- Decline permanently for this device

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/signup-05-biometric-setup.png"
    alt="Biometric authentication setup prompt"
  />
</Frame>

<Note>
This feature enhances security while making future logins faster. You can enable it later from your account settings.
</Note>

### Step 5: Verify Your Email

RubixKube requires email verification to ensure account security. You'll see a confirmation screen indicating:

- **Verification email sent**to your email address
-  Instructions to check your inbox
-  Option to resend the verification email

<Frame>
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/getting-started/signup-06-email-verification-required.png"
    alt="Email verification required screen"
  />
</Frame>

### What to do next:
1.**Check your email inbox**(including spam/junk folders)
2.**Click the verification link**in the email from RubixKube
3.**Return to this page**and click**"Continue (After Verification)"**
<Accordion title="Didn't receive the email?">
  If you don't see the verification email after a few minutes:
  
  - Check your**spam or junk folder**- Ensure you entered the correct email address
  - Click**"Resend Email"**on the verification page
  - Wait 2-3 minutes and check again
  - Contact support at connect@rubixkube.ai if issues persist
</Accordion>

## Social Login Options

### GitHub Signup

1. Click**"Continue with GitHub"**on the signup page
2. Authorize RubixKube to access your GitHub account
3. You'll be automatically logged in (no email verification needed)
4. GitHub email will be used for your RubixKube account

<Tip>
**Perfect for developers:**GitHub signup is the fastest way to get started if you already use GitHub for work.
</Tip>

### Google Signup

1. Click**"Continue with Google"**on the signup page
2. Select your Google account from the picker
3. Grant RubixKube the necessary permissions
4. You'll be automatically logged in
5. Your Google workspace email will be used

<Tip>
**Enterprise-friendly:**Ideal if your organization uses Google Workspace.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email verification link expired">
    Verification links expire after 24 hours for security. Simply:
    1. Return to the signup page
    2. Click "Resend Email"
    3. Use the new verification link
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password doesn't meet requirements">
    Ensure your password has:
    - At least 8 characters
    - Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
    - At least one number
    - At least one special character (!, @, #, $, etc.)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="OAuth (GitHub/Google) login not working">
    Common issues:
    - **Pop-up blocked:**Allow pop-ups for console.rubixkube.ai
    - **Organization restrictions:**Your GitHub org may restrict OAuth apps
    - **Google workspace policies:**Check with your IT admin
    
    If social login fails, use email signup as an alternative.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access account after signup">
    Ensure you:
    1. Verified your email address
    2. Used the correct email/password combination
    3. Are not behind a VPN that blocks access
    
    Try the "Forgot Password" option if you can't remember your credentials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
