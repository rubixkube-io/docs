## Step 1: Choose your sign‑up method

On the RubixKube sign‑up portal you have three options: sign up with GitHub, sign up with Google, or create an account directly. Select **Create an account** to register with your email and organisation.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/d891ad7c-9546-4428-a3e7-0a2cfa85de6f.png"
  alt="RubixKube sign‑up options"
/>

## Step 2: Provide your organisation and accept terms

On the **Join RubixKube** screen:

- Enter your organisation or team name (optional). If left blank, RubixKube will generate a name automatically.
- Read and agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
- Click **Create account** to proceed, or choose GitHub or Google sign‑up if you prefer.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/53f53fb4-5446-444b-b5f2-e4c07f218803.png"
  alt="Join RubixKube form"
/>

<Warning>
Use only non‑production details during Alpha. Do not sign up with production cluster names or sensitive information.
</Warning>

## Step 3: Enter your email

Next, enter your work email address and click **Continue**. This verifies that the account belongs to you.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/c49030d3-0584-41ef-b287-58b9f5be309d.png"
  alt="Email entry form"
/>

## Step 4: Set your password

You will be asked to choose a strong password. It must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least three of the following character types: lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers and special characters.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/6ecabe9f-7ba3-4a8a-bf6b-e5c37f68f991.png"
  alt="Password requirements"
/>

<Note>
Keep your password safe. Do not share it. If you suspect it’s compromised, reset it immediately.
</Note>

## Step 5: Enable quick login (optional)

RubixKube may prompt you to enable quick login using your device’s fingerprint or face recognition. This step is optional. You can choose **Continue** to enable it, **Remind me later**, or **Not on this device** to decline.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/49b93ed8-bf9f-4042-a3a4-b9d02a3096e3.png"
  alt="Quick login prompt"
/>

## After sign‑up

Once your account is created, you’ll land on the RubixKube dashboard. To start monitoring your infrastructure, connect your first cluster by running the `kubectl apply` command shown on the page. This installs the RubixKube observer agent in your cluster.
