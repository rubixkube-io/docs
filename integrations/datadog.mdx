## Why integrate, rather than replace

Datadog does what it does well. RubixKube is not trying to become a Datadog replacement. What we are building is a correlation layer that joins Datadog signals with infrastructure observation, memory, and autonomous recommendation, producing one evidence graph instead of two parallel stories.
