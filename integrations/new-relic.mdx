## Why integrate, rather than replace

New Relic does what it does well. RubixKube is not trying to become an APM vendor. What we are building is a correlation layer that joins your APM signals with infrastructure observation, memory, and autonomous recommendation, producing one evidence graph instead of two parallel stories.
