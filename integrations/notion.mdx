Connect Notion to publish RCA reports into your team wiki. Useful for post-mortem workflows, knowledge sharing, and turning every investigation into durable documentation.

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="RCA to Notion page" icon="file-lines">
    Publish any RCA report as a Notion page, formatted and linked back to the source incident.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Post-mortem workflow" icon="clipboard-list">
    Pair with Linear to turn RCAs into tickets and post-mortems in one click.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skill-powered sync" icon="sparkles">
    The `linear-tickets-to-notion` system skill brings a weekly incident digest into Notion automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Database integration" icon="database">
    Write into a structured Notion database (status, severity, service, owner) for reporting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Install

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Create a Notion integration">
    In Notion, go to **Settings → Integrations → Develop your own integrations** and create an internal integration. Copy the token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share pages or a database">
    In Notion, open the pages or database you want RubixKube to write into and **Add connection → RubixKube**. The integration only sees what you explicitly share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure in RubixKube">
    In the console, open **Integrations → Notion**, paste the token, and pick the default page or database for RCA publication.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What a published RCA looks like in Notion

- **Title** matching the RCA headline.
- **Callout** at the top linking back to the RubixKube report.
- **Observed conditions**, **Causal chain**, and **Recommended actions** as sections.
- **Evidence** inline as links to the source signals in RubixKube.
- **Properties** (when writing to a database): severity, environment, service, status.

## Using the system skill

Once connected, enable the `linear-tickets-to-notion` system skill from the Skills catalogue. It fetches your assigned Linear issues and writes them into a new Notion page, typically used for weekly incident reviews. See [Agent Skills Store](/using/agent-skills-store).

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Publish fails with 'not found'">
    The Notion integration cannot see a page or database unless you explicitly share it. Open the target in Notion and add the RubixKube connection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pages missing properties">
    When publishing into a database, RubixKube needs the database schema to match (severity, environment, service, status). Add missing properties or adjust the mapping in **Integrations → Notion → Schema**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
