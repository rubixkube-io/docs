## Why integrate, rather than replace

Sentry does what it does well. RubixKube is not trying to become a Sentry replacement. What we are building is a correlation layer that joins Sentry signals with infrastructure observation, memory, and autonomous recommendation, producing one evidence graph instead of two parallel stories.
