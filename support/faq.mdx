# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is RubixKube?
An AI-assisted Site Reliability Intelligence platform for Kubernetes, AWS, GCP, and VM environments.

### What browsers are supported?
Latest Chrome, Edge, Safari, and Firefox.

---

## Setup

### How do I connect my first environment?
Choose your path from [Environment Quick Starts](/getting-started/environment-quickstarts). Kubernetes environments use `kubectl` install, while AWS/GCP/VM environments use the observer installer script.

### How long until data shows up?
Within 1-2 minutes after installation, depending on environment size.

---

## Insights & RCA

### How are incidents detected?
RubixKube continuously analyzes infrastructure signals (events, logs, metrics) to detect abnormal patterns.

### Can I trust the RCA?
RCA includes confidence levels and evidence so you can validate conclusions.

---

## Agents

### Which agents are required?
Observer, RCA Pipeline, and SRI. Optional: Memory Engine and MCP server.

### What if an agent is inactive?
For Kubernetes: check `rubixkube-system` namespace pods and logs. For VM/cloud environments: validate observer service status and credentials. See the Troubleshooting guide.

---

## Billing & Security

### Do you store application data?
No. The observer collects metadata and metrics; not application payloads.

### What auth methods are supported?
SSO via Auth0 (Google/GitHub); optional backup password.

---

## Documentation

### Images not appearing in docs?
Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R), ensure images are committed under `/images/using/`, and re-deploy docs.

### Where can I get more help?
Use the Troubleshooting guide or contact support with Tenant ID, repro steps, and screenshots.
