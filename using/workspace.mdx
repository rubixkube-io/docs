# Managing Workspace & Teams

The **Workspace** page lets you invite team members, assign roles, and manage your organization's access to RubixKube.

---

## User Roles

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="crown">
    **Full access:**
    - Add/remove users
    - Manage clusters
    - Change settings
    - View all data
  </Card>

  <Card title="Member" icon="user">
    **Standard access:**
    - View all data
    - Use Chat
    - View RCA reports
    - Cannot change settings
  </Card>

  <Card title="Viewer" icon="eye">
    **Read-only:**
    - View dashboards
    - Read RCA reports
    - Cannot modify anything
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Invite User">
    Enter email address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Role">
    Choose Admin, Member, or Viewer
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    They receive email with signup link
  </Step>

  <Step title="They Accept">
    User appears in your workspace
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Managing Users

**View all users:**
- See name, email, role
- Last active time
- Status (active/pending)

**Change roles:**
- Click user → Edit role
- Promote/demote as needed

**Remove users:**
- Click user → Remove
- Access revoked immediately
